Fixed Asset Management... SIMPLIFIED!

What is it?

A cloud based all-inclusive Fixed Assets Management Solution that automates the entire life cycle of a fixed asset from commission to retirement and provides complete visibility to the users. The system digitizes end-to-end lifecycle of the assets and acts as a repository for all the documents.

Features

Digitize Asset Management

Digitize the all the records, documents, and processes and approvals.

Tracking

Track the movement and custodian of the asset throughout the lifecycle of an asset.

Tagging

Create barcodes, QR Codes, and RFIDs through the system based on predefined logics to suit your needs.

Audit

Conduct the audits by scanning the barcodes, QR Code and RFID affixed on fixed assets through a mobile application and export the reconciliation report.

Movement Management

Facilitates the process of movement of assets between group companies, locations, and departments through comprehensive documentation and approval process.

Disposition

Manage asset disposals through the system.
